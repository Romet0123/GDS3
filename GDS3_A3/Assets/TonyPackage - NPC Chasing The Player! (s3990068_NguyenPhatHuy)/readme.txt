1. Go to Window -> Package Manager -> Unity Registry -> AI Navigation -> Install (If you have not installed AI Navigation)

2. Go to the TonySampleScene (scene) to take a look at the sample. (Ensure that you are using URP!)
Note: Press Left Shift to Run and Left Control to Crouch!

3. In your own game, just add the TonyKiller.cs to your NPC. You can analyze the code of TonyKiller.cs to see how the logic works! 

4. Remember to implement the navMeshAgent to your NPC and the NavMeshSurface to your map. (Sample is in TonySampleScene)
Take a look at these video to have more understanding on NavMeshAgent and NavMeshSurface.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SMWxCpLvrcc
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=u2EQtrdgfNs

5. Set The Tag and Layer of your player as "Player"!
Note: In terms of playerMovement, you can use your own movement script as long as your player has the tag and layer "Player"!
